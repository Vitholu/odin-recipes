Easy Cake Pops

Tasty, easy-making, and perfect for kids.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix (such as Betty Crocker®)
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • lollipop sticks
  • 1 (14 ounce) bag chocolate confectioners' coating
